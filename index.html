About

I'm an engineering student at EFREI Paris, currently specializing in embedded systems and robotics, following an exchange semester at Concordia University in Montreal.

My background started in aerospace: two years at IPSA Paris gave me a solid grounding in mechanics, structures, and thermodynamics. A technical internship at EDF, inside a nuclear plant's safety-validation process, taught me what operational rigor actually looks like. That's the standard I try to apply to every system I build since.

I hold a private pilot license in progress (PPL, Cessna 172), which now feeds directly into my current project: an autonomous drone built to visually inspect light aircraft.
